DUCK VERSE ARENA

A ridiculous browser fighting prototype. Two ducks enter. One duck quacks.

Playable today. Built to evolve.

How to Play

Desktop: Player 1 uses A/D to move, W to jump, F punch, G special.

Desktop: Player 2 uses arrow keys to move, ↑ jump, / punch, . special.

Mobile: Use the on-screen buttons.
